Welcome to Swap

Swap is service built to improve the education experience for both students and instructors by making sure students have the correct equipments they require for their courses.

Instructors can help students prepare for their course by listing the equipments they'll need to succeed. Swap allows instructors to keep track of which students are on progress in preparation for their course.

Students can see the equipments they'll need beforehand for a course and have convient ways to acquire them. With Swap, students can trade with other students by exchanging items they have from previous courses.
